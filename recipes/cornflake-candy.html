Cornflake Candy

Cornflake Candy- a sweet sticky treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of Karo corn syrup
  • 8 cups of cornflakes
  1. Heat corn syrup and sugar in large pot while stirring, until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Turn off heat and stir in peanut butter.
  3. Add cornflakes a bit at a time, stirring to coat cornflakes.
  4. Spread mixture on a greased or lined pan.
  5. Eat immediately or wait to cool.
